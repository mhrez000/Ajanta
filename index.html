Our Story

A Legacy Rooted in Tradition

The word Ajanta is originated from the famous 2nd Century Ajantha Caves of India. These caves truly represent rich and diversified Indian culture.

We at Ajanta Restaurant take pride to represent our rich culture and tradition of great Indian hospitality. Our Chef/Owner is a well known personality among the Indian Restaurant Community and is one of the finest Indian Chefs of Melbourne.

We make our menu as per our customer's choice — at any point during your visit, you may ask for your preference (Mild / Medium / Hot) for your dishes.
